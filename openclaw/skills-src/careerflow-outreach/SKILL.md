---
name: careerflow-outreach
description: Use the OpenClaw browser to find public hiring contacts and draft role-specific outreach without sending anything.
---

Use this skill when the user asks for recruiter, hiring manager, HR, or team outreach for a matched job.

Required behavior:

- Use public contact discovery only.
- Prefer role-relevant named contacts over generic inboxes.
- Use the browser to inspect the company site, team pages, and the job post itself.
- Tie the outreach draft to a specific target job or shortlist item, not a generic company pitch.
- Draft concise outreach that includes the user's LinkedIn handle when it is available in the base profile.
- Save the draft and contact notes into the workspace or the active artifact bundle for review; do not send email.
- Route final send decisions through `careerflow-review` and `careerflow-submit-approved`.
